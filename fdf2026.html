Geant4-driven realistic waveform generation for FPGA algorithm verification in radiation detector readout

This page presents my featured poster for the 3rd FPGA Developers’ Forum at CERN.

Overview

The work proposes a workflow connecting detector physics simulation, event-level data, waveform generation, and verification-oriented digital processing for FPGA algorithm studies. The goal is to create realistic signal representations derived from physical detector interactions.

Keywords

Geant4, detector simulation, waveform generation, FPGA verification, detector readout, signal modeling
