About

Background and Research Interests

I am a Physics Engineer with a strong interest in experimental physics, radiation detection, and Monte Carlo-based computational modeling. My work brings together simulation, measurement, and data analysis to better understand detector systems and develop physics-based solutions for real-world applications. I am especially motivated by interdisciplinary research that connects theory, experiment, and technology.
