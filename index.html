I am a Physics Engineer working at the intersection of experimental physics, detector systems, Monte Carlo simulations, and scientific computing. My work includes Geant4-based modeling, radiation measurements, detector-oriented data analysis, and interdisciplinary engineering applications.

This website presents my academic profile, selected projects, conference participation, featured poster work, and research-related activities.

In a nutshell

  • I work on radiation detection, detector systems, and applied nuclear instrumentation.
  • I use Geant4, computational modeling, and experimental validation together.
  • My interests include neutron and gamma-ray studies, shielding, waveform workflows, and scientific reporting.
  • I aim to build a research profile connecting physics, engineering, and real-world applications.
